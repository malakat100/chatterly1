Safety Tips

Stay safe while chatting on Chatterly

Online Safety Basics

Chatterly is designed to be a safe and friendly environment for everyone. However, it's important to follow these basic safety guidelines to protect yourself while chatting online.

Protect Your Personal Information

Never share sensitive personal information such as:

  • Full name
  • Home address
  • Phone number
  • Financial information
  • Social media accounts

Be Cautious with Strangers

Remember that people online may not be who they claim to be. Be cautious when:

  • Meeting someone in person
  • Sharing photos or videos
  • Accepting files or links
  • Making financial transactions

Report Inappropriate Behavior

If you encounter harassment, threats, or inappropriate content:

  • Use the "Report User" button in chat
  • Block the user immediately
  • Contact our support team
  • Save evidence if necessary

Account Security

Keep your account secure by:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Not sharing your account with others
  • Logging out when using shared devices
  • Being cautious with third-party apps

Avoid Scams and Fraud

Be aware of common online scams:

  • Requests for money or financial assistance
  • Investment opportunities that sound too good to be true
  • Requests to click on suspicious links
  • Attempts to gain access to your accounts

Parental Guidance

If you're a parent or guardian, we recommend:

  • Discussing online safety with your children
  • Setting up parental controls on devices
  • Monitoring your child's online activity
  • Teaching them to recognize and avoid online risks

Need Help?

If you feel unsafe or need assistance, please contact our support team immediately at support@chatterly.com or use our Contact Us page.
